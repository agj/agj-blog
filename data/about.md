Hey! I'm Ale Grilli, known online as agj. I'm a programmer who studied graphic design and new media art, and likes languages and writing, among many other things. This is my blog, and here I write whatever I feel like writing. It acts mostly as a record for my experiences and for the things I make myself, such as little games, videos or writing. I write a lot of technical coding stuff, more recently.

You may contact me by email via ale¶agj.cl or on [Mastodon][mastodon]. Check the [/colophon](/colophon) page for more about how this website was made. You may also visit [my homepage][home].
